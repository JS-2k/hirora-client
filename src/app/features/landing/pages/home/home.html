Interviews that feel like interviews

[ How it works ]

Four clear steps to your next hire.

01

Post the role

Add the job title, must-have skills, salary range, location, and the kind of person you actually want to meet. The clearer the brief, the better the shortlist.

02

Hirora ranks the pipeline

Each candidate is scored against the role with a short explanation, so your team can see why someone is a strong fit instead of guessing from keywords.

03

Invite the right people

Review the top matches and approve the ones you want to interview. Hirora handles the first round, and candidates can answer when it works for them.

04

Read the report

Get the recommendation, match score, interview summary, strengths, and concerns in one place. You can move forward with confidence, or pass with a clear reason.
